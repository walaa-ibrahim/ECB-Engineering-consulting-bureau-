Organizational Structure

Our strongest asset is the people who make up our team

We are structured in a way that allows us to take on any one of our services or a combined service form each office. Our structure allows for a Director or Associate Director led commission, working across our service specialism if necessary to allow our Clients a single point of contact. We believe in resourcing the scope, not the fee.

OOur people are used to working in a collaborative environment and we structure our teams in a way that allows for support and development through our people s careers. We are passionate about staff retention and development and use our annual and interim appraisals to assess our people’s needs.

Our people know that delivering our service is more than just doing the job. It is important that our teams understand your needs and aim at every opportunity to enjoy delivering our service and be proud of our commissions.
